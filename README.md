By coupling an ACID-compliant MySQL backend with mathematical Operations Research (OR) models, the system dynamically calculates risk, automates inventory health classification, and generates exact, mathematically optimized replenishment schedules in real-time.📈 Data Analytics & Operations Research FrameworkThe core engine utilizes three classic Operations Research and Supply Chain models to optimize thousands of SKUs dynamically:1. Safety Stock ModelingTo prevent stockouts caused by upstream delays or sudden demand spikes, the system establishes a dynamic buffer using a standard normal distribution for a 95% service level:$$Safety\ Stock = 1.65 \times \sigma \times \sqrt{L}$$Where:$\sigma$ = Standard deviation of daily demand$L$ = Supplier lead time (days)2. Dynamic Reorder Point (ROP)Rather than using arbitrary static thresholds, the replenishment trigger adapts continuously to historical consumption velocities:$$ROP = (Avg\ Daily\ Demand \times L) + Safety\ Stock$$The system monitors stock balances and flags an automated warning the moment physical inventory dips below this calculated metric.3. Economic Order Quantity (EOQ)To minimize the total carrying cost and ordering cost, the system calculates the mathematically optimal batch size to purchase using the Wilson Formula:$$EOQ = \sqrt{\frac{2DS}{H}}$$Where:$D$ = Annualized demand volume$S$ = Flat administrative cost per purchase order$H$ = Annual holding cost per unit📊 Analytical Features & Business Intelligence🧬 Algorithmic ABC Inventory ClassificationInstead of manually sorting products, the system applies the Pareto Principle (80/20 Rule) over a rolling 90-day revenue window to automatically segment inventory:SegmentRevenue ContributionOperational StrategyClass ATop 80% of Cumulative ValueTight Control: High-frequency cycle counts, stringent supplier SLAs, minimized safety stocks.Class BNext 15% of Cumulative ValueModerate Control: Periodic reviews, automated standard reordering.Class CBottom 5% of Cumulative ValueMinimal Oversight: Bulk ordering, loose controls to reduce administrative overhead.🛠️ Live Inventory Health SegmentationEvery SKU is categorized in real-time by cross-referencing live stock levels against the calculated OR metrics:🔴 CRITICAL: Inventory has breached the safety buffer; immediate stockout risk.🟡 WARNING: Current stock is below the ROP; replenishment orders must be dispatched.🟢 HEALTHY: Inventory levels are optimized within safe boundaries.📊 Demand & Supplier Performance IntelligenceTime-Series Visualizations: Interactive Plotly dashboards charting monthly revenue trends to isolate structural seasonal shifts from organic baseline growth.Category Health Aggregations: Macro-level breakdowns mapping healthy vs. breaking categories to inform executive procurement budgets.🗄️ System Architecture & Data EngineeringThe system balances heavy analytical processing with data integrity by decoupling analytics from transactional write operations.Data Schema & Pipeline Architectureproject_back/
